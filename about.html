About Gans & Smith

Over 135 years of protecting East Texas families and businesses — with integrity, knowledge, trust, community, and loyalty at the heart of everything we do.

Our History

A Legacy Built on Trust

Gans & Smith Insurance Agency, Inc. was organized in 1889 by J.R. and T.E. Clemmons, and has operated continuously during this period, with certain name changes. The agency assumed its present name in 1912, under the partnership of W. Bruner Smith and Daniel W. Gans.

There have been no name changes since, though several partners have come and gone during this time. Ownership of the agency at present is vested in four corporate principals.

We may be over 100 years old, but we're not living in the past — we are looking to the future with great anticipation. Gans & Smith has faith in the future of the American Agency System and the independent agent.
